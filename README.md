# 📊 Electronic Shop Sales Insights Dashboard

# 🔎 Project Overview

This project analyzes an electronic retail store dataset to understand sales performance, product demand, and regional profitability using Tableau.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales trends across cities, products, and states, helping identify high-performing products, low-performing locations, and pricing patterns within the electronic shop portfolio.

# 🎯 Business Objective

The goal of this analysis is to:

Identify cities with the lowest number of orders

Analyze product demand across different quarters

Evaluate profit distribution by state

Understand product pricing behavior

Identify top-performing products based on quantity ordered

Provide interactive insights to support sales and product strategy decisions

# 🏗️ Data Model

The analysis is built on a structured dataset containing sales transactions for an electronics store.

Key fields used:

Order Date

Product Name

Quantity Ordered

Price Each

Purchaser Address

Derived Fields Created:

City (Extracted from Purchaser Address)

State (First two letters from address)

Order Total (Quantity Ordered × Price Each)

Order Profit (25% of Order Total)

Quarter (Extracted from Order Date)

Year, Month, and Day columns

These fields enable time-based analysis and regional insights.

# 📌 Key KPIs

Total Orders

Total Sales

Average Price

Total Profit

These KPIs provide a quick overview of business performance.

# 📄 Dashboard Structure

### Sales Overview

High-level sales performance across cities and products.

Bottom 5 cities by number of orders

Average price above $200 by product name

Top 4 products by quantity ordered in Q4

Interactive filters for City, State, Product Name, and Quantity

# 📊 Key Insights

San Francisco generates the highest sales volume among all cities.

Macbook Pro Laptop has the highest average product price among high-value products.

Charging cables and batteries appear among the most frequently ordered products in Q4.

Profit distribution shows strong contributions from specific states such as Texas and Washington.

Some cities generate significantly fewer orders, highlighting potential areas for marketing or expansion.

Download the Tableau workbook (.twbx) from this repository.

Analyze product demand, pricing trends, and profitability across regions.
